Student and Activities

Social Activities

"Udaan", the social initiative for Techspardha'18 aims at empowering the women by generating awareness regarding 'Mensurational Hygiene' in the rural areas. Under the initiative 'Udaan' 15+ villages are to be visited to spread awareness among rural women. Under the able sponsorship of Paree, the program commenced on 12th January 2018, in village Mirzapur with great enthusiasm. On 16th January 2018, the campaign was fruitfully concluded in Govt. Higher Secondary School, Jyotisar with an aware audience and free distribution of sanitary pads among women
